What is HerHealth?


HerHealth is your all-in-one wellness companion built to support and empower girls and women through every phase of their cycle. With a smartPeriod Tracker, interactivedashboard, and a growing library of self-care tips, it helps you log, understand, and care for your body with ease. From tracking symptoms to offering guidance on mental and physical well-being, HerHealth makes your menstrual journey feel understood, organized, and beautifully supported.
